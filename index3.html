NuVision: Empowering Healthier Eating with AI

LLM + Vision + iOS (2025)

The Challenge

In Hong Kong, many families in subdivided flats face difficulties preparing nutritious meals due to limited cooking space, leading to reliance on takeout. NuVision addresses this by providing an AI-powered mobile app for instant nutritional analysis of meals from a single photo.

Key Features

  • Instant Food Recognition via Photo
  • Detailed Nutritional Breakdown
  • Personalized Dietary Recommendations
  • Progress Tracking (Daily, Weekly, Monthly)

Privacy First: Your Data, Your Control

NuVision is designed with your privacy as a top priority. Key privacy features include:

  • Completely Free & Ad-Free
  • Local Data Storage on your iOS device
  • Data Encryption for locally stored data
  • User Data Ownership: Export your data anytime
  • No Identifiable Personal Information Collected

Pass Secure: iOS Password Manager

SwiftData + iOS (2024)

Secure & Seamless

Pass Secure simplifies password management using SwiftData and iCloudKit. Credentials are securely protected on-device and synchronized across your iOS devices signed in with the same iCloud account.

Security Features

  • Biometrics (Face ID/Fingerprint) or Passcode Authentication
  • iCloud Sync for Passcode Records
  • Encrypted PDF Export & Plain CSV Export
  • Session Timeout (5 minutes)
  • Keyword Search Functionality
