NuVision

Empowering Healthier Eating with AI

Transform Your Nutrition with NuVision

In Hong Kong, limited cooking spaces in subdivided flats make it challenging for families to maintain proper nutrition, especially when relying on takeout. NuVision, a free AI-powered app by Caritas Social Work Services Division, analyzes meal nutrition from a single photo, helping underprivileged communities eat healthier.

Download Now

Key Features

Instant Nutritional Analysis

Snap a photo, and NuVision instantly provides a detailed breakdown of calories, carbohydrates, protein, and fat. It recognizes hundreds of Chinese and Western dishes, estimating portion sizes and cooking methods.

Personalized Recommendations

Get tailored dietary advice based on your age, gender, and body weight to support your health goals.

Track Your Progress

Monitor daily intake with daily, weekly, and monthly trend reports to stay on track toward healthier eating habits.

Privacy First

Designed for underprivileged communities, NuVision is free, ad-free, and prioritizes your privacy. All data is stored locally on your iOS device with encryption, and you can export it easily for backup or analysis. No personal information is collected.
